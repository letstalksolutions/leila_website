NADJA Media

As co-founder of NADJA Media, I work to highlight women's achievements and contributions in a media landscape where female voices are often underrepresented.

NADJA is a digital news platform dedicated to publishing news and features that address gender bias in media, where only 25% of news stories worldwide feature women as experts and spokespeople.
