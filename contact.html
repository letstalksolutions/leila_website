Frequently Asked Questions

What types of training do you offer?

I offer a range of training programs including "What's Behind the Headlines?" for students, "How to Write for Impact" for aspiring writers, and personalized mentorship for journalists at any career stage. I also develop custom programs for organizations and educational institutions.

Where do training sessions take place?

Training can be conducted in-person in London or virtually via video conferencing platforms. For organizations and schools, I can travel to your location depending on availability.

How much does training cost?

Pricing varies depending on the program, duration, and number of participants. Please contact me with your specific requirements for a customized quote.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, discounted rates are available for group bookings and educational institutions. Please mention the number of participants in your inquiry.

How long are the training programs?

Program duration varies. "What's Behind the Headlines?" is typically a 5-week course with weekly sessions. "How to Write for Impact" can be delivered as a series of workshops or an intensive course. Mentorship programs can be structured according to your needs.

Can you create a custom program for my organization?

Absolutely! I specialize in developing bespoke training programs tailored to the specific needs and objectives of organizations, schools, and media outlets.
