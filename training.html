Training Programs

Journalism Training for the Modern Media Landscape

My training programs are designed to equip aspiring journalists with the practical skills and critical thinking abilities needed to navigate today's complex media environment. Whether you're a student just starting out or a writer looking to refine your craft, I offer customized training to help you achieve your goals.

All programs can be tailored to individual needs or delivered to groups in educational or professional settings.

What's Behind the Headlines?

Media Literacy for Students

A 5-week course designed to equip students with essential media literacy skills and the fundamentals of journalism. This program is particularly effective for students aged 14-15 but can be adapted for different age groups.

Program Objectives:

  • Critically analyze social media and news content
  • Identify misinformation and disinformation
  • Verify sources and detect bias in reporting
  • Plan and structure a news story
  • Write compelling headlines and introductions

Program Structure:

Session 1: Social Media - Can You Spot Fake News?

Learn to identify fake stories, understand the difference between misinformation and disinformation, and develop a checklist for verifying content.

Session 2: How to Write a News Story

Analyze current news articles, learn the 5Ws/inverted pyramid structure, and explore different types of articles.

Session 3: Can You Tell AI Apart from Humans?

Spot AI-generated text and images, understand the impact of AI on news, and learn to use AI ethically for ideas.

Session 4: Checking Your Sources

Learn what source verification means, how to fact-check your work, and how to identify bias in reporting.

Session 5: What's Behind the Headlines?

Analyze language used in headlines, learn to write headlines that hook readers, and develop an editing checklist for your articles.

How to Write for Impact

Flagship Program for Aspiring Writers

My flagship program designed for both aspiring writers and experienced journalists. This comprehensive training provides practical tools to write impactful stories and builds the confidence needed to pitch ideas to commissioning editors.

Program Objectives:

  • Develop a distinctive writing voice
  • Structure compelling narratives
  • Research and verify information effectively
  • Create headlines and introductions that capture attention
  • Build confidence in pitching ideas to publications

Program Components:

Story Development Workshop

Learn to identify newsworthy angles and develop ideas into fully-formed stories.

Writing Techniques Masterclass

Explore narrative structures, pacing, and language techniques that engage readers.

Research and Verification Training

Develop skills to research thoroughly and verify information from multiple sources.

Pitching Workshop

Learn how to craft effective pitches and build relationships with editors.

Personalized Feedback Sessions

Receive detailed feedback on your writing and specific guidance for improvement.

This program can be delivered as a series of workshops or as an intensive course, depending on your needs and schedule.

One-on-One Mentorship

Personalized Guidance for Journalists

Tailored mentorship for journalists at any stage of their career. This program provides individualized support to help you develop your skills, overcome challenges, and achieve your professional goals.

Mentorship Benefits:

  • Personalized feedback on your writing and reporting
  • Guidance on career development and specialization
  • Support with pitching to publications
  • Advice on building your portfolio and personal brand
  • Strategies for navigating industry challenges

Mentorship Options:

Single Session

A one-time consultation to address specific questions or review a particular piece of work.

Monthly Mentorship

Regular monthly sessions to provide ongoing support and guidance for your development.

Intensive Mentorship

A structured program with weekly sessions over a 3-month period, designed to accelerate your growth.

All mentorship programs are tailored to your specific needs and goals, with flexible scheduling to accommodate your availability.
