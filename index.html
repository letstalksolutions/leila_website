Journalism Training for the Modern Media Landscape

As a London-based journalist and editor specializing in human rights, social justice, and culture, I've dedicated my career to ethical journalism and media literacy. Co-founder of NADJA Media, I now focus on training aspiring journalists to navigate today's complex media environment with confidence and integrity.

My bespoke training programs equip students with practical skills to identify misinformation, write compelling stories, and develop critical thinking abilities essential for modern journalism.

Why Media Literacy Matters

In a world where 94% of people in the UK have witnessed misinformation on social media, and 46% of children aged 12-15 struggle to identify false news stories, media literacy has never been more important.

My training programs are designed to address this critical gap, equipping the next generation of journalists with the skills to navigate the complex media landscape with integrity and purpose.
